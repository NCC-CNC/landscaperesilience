Endemic Species Richness counts the unique Endemic Species occurance map that intersect each 1km grid unit.

Endemic Species Goal totals the required protection goal of each Endemic species occurrence map. Goals are calculated using methods from Rodrigues et al, 2004. to ensure species persistence throughout time.

Unit:
Richness: species count.
Goal: cumulative sum.

Source:
Individual species occurrence maps for a 10x10km grid provided by NatureServe.
