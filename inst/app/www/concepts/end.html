Endemic Species Richness counts the number of unique Endemic Species occurances that intersect each 1km grid unit.

Endemic Species Goal totals the required protection goal of each Endemic species occurrence area. Goals are calculated using methods from Rodrigues et al (2004) to ensure species persistence throughout time.

Unit
Richness, species count.
Goal, cumulative sum.

Source
NatureServe Canada and NCC (occurrences mapped to a 10km grid)
