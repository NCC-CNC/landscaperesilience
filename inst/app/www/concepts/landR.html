Landscape Resilience Score adds ecological variables that capture themes of biodiversity, connectivity, climate, habitat, and threats at the landscape scale.

Landscape Resilience Score =
Critical Habitat + Species at Risk Richness + Endemic Species Richness + Common Species Richness + Species at Risk Goal + Endemic Species Goal + Common Species Goal + Connectivity + Climate Centrality + Climate Refugia + Forest Landcover + Grassland + Wetland + River + Shoreline - Climate Extremes - Human Footprint

Variables are min-max normalized between 0-1 before adding.

The Contribution to Resilience captures the proportion input variables have on the Landscape Resilience Score within a project boundary.

The Adjusted Landscape Resilience Score divides Landscape Resilience by the project area.
