Species at Risk Richness counts the uniques SAR range map extents that intersect each 1km grid unit.

Species at Risk Goal totals the required protection goal of each unique SAR range map extent. Goals are calculated using methods from Rodrigues et al, 2004. to ensure species persistence throughout time.

Unit:
Richness: species count.
Goal: cumulative sum.

Source:
NatureServe data, SAR recovery strategies, Environment Canada resources and COSEWIC status reports.
